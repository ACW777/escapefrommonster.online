About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an adrenaline-pumping action game where you face waves of increasingly difficult monsters. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while upgrading your character and unlocking new abilities.

Inspired by classic roguelike games, Monster Survivors combines fast-paced combat with strategic decision-making. Each playthrough offers a unique experience as you collect different power-ups and face randomized enemy patterns.

The game features beautiful pixel art graphics, immersive sound effects, and an addictive gameplay loop that will keep you coming back for "just one more run"!

Game Features

Fast-Paced Action

Experience non-stop action with hordes of monsters attacking from all directions. Quick reflexes and strategic movement are key to survival.

Progression System

Level up your character and unlock powerful new abilities. Choose your upgrades wisely to create the ultimate monster-slaying build.

Multiple Characters

Choose from different characters, each with unique starting abilities and playstyles. Master them all to find your favorite survival strategy.

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Your character attacks automatically when enemies are in range
  • Press ESC to pause the game
  • Select upgrades with your mouse when leveling up

Tips for Beginners

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters
  • Collect experience gems to level up faster
  • Focus on a few complementary abilities rather than spreading too thin
  • Some enemies drop health when defeated - prioritize these when low on health
  • The longer you survive, the stronger you become - but so do the monsters!
